Your Privacy at CineMax

At CineMax, your privacy is a cornerstone of your cinematic experience. This policy details how we collect, use, and protect your information as you discover movies, build watchlists, and stream trailers using our platform. By using CineMax, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. Information We Collect

Based on your interaction with CineMax, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Information: If you register, we may collect your name and email address.
  • Watch History & Preferences: Movies/TV shows you watch, search queries, and items added to your wishlist (stored locally).
  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device identifiers, and interaction data (e.g., scroll, clicks).
  • Cookies & Local Storage: We use localStorage to remember your wishlist, continue-watching progress, and cache movie data for faster loading (as implemented in app.js).
  • Performance Data: Anonymous latency measurements for our streaming servers to provide you with the fastest playback (server latency checks).

All data is collected to personalize your experience and improve platform performance.

2. How We Use Your Information

CineMax utilizes your data for the following purposes, directly reflected in our application logic:

  • Personalization: To power features like "Continue Watching," "AI Recommendations," and your personal wishlist.
  • Analytics & Optimization: To understand which content is popular, optimize server selection (fastest server detection), and improve caching strategies.
  • Service Delivery: To stream trailers, display movie posters, and fetch metadata from TMDB based on your searches.
  • Security: To monitor for unauthorized access and ensure the integrity of our platform.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Data stored locally (wishlist, watch history) remains on your device unless you choose to share it.

3. Third-Party Services

CineMax integrates with the following third-party services to deliver content:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB): We fetch movie metadata, posters, trailers, and ratings using their API. Your searches and requests are sent to TMDB; their privacy policy applies to those transmissions.
  • Streaming Servers (Vidlux): When you play a movie or episode, your request is routed through our server selection logic to third-party streaming hosts. These services may collect viewing data according to their own policies.
  • YouTube (for trailers): Trailers are embedded via YouTube; Google’s privacy policy governs your interaction with those players.
  • Analytics: We may use aggregated, anonymized analytics to understand platform usage; no personal data is shared.

CineMax is not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their policies.

4. Local Storage & Cookies

Our application uses your browser's localStorage to save:

  • Your wishlist (cinemax_wishlist)
  • Continue-watching progress (cinemax_continue_watching)
  • Watch history (cinemax_watch_history)
  • Cached movie data (cinemax_cache) to reduce API calls and speed up browsing
  • "New content" markers (cinemax_new_content)

This data stays on your device and is never automatically transmitted to our servers. You can clear it at any time via your browser settings. No tracking cookies are used for cross-site advertising.

5. Data Retention

We retain cached API data for up to one hour (configurable) to ensure fresh content. Your local wishlist and history are stored indefinitely on your device until you clear them. Server logs (if any) containing IP addresses are anonymized after 30 days.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures, including HTTPS encryption and input sanitization, to protect data in transit. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You acknowledge that you use CineMax at your own risk.

7. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your locally stored data (you can clear browser storage).
  • Opt out of any future data collection (by ceasing use of the platform).

Since most data is stored locally, you have direct control via your browser's developer tools. For any server-side data inquiries, please contact us.

8. Children's Privacy

CineMax is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of CineMax after changes constitutes acceptance.
